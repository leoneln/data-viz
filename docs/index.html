Welcome to Data Visualization

Course Introduction

Data Visualization is both an art and a science. It's the graphical representation of information, but it's also about transforming complex data into compelling narratives that capture attention and ignite action. This course delves into the power of data visualization to unlock insights from overwhelming datasets. You'll learn how to leverage the creativity of design principles alongside the accuracy of data analysis to craft effective visualizations that resonate with your audience.

This course equips you with the skills to transform raw data into clear, concise, and impactful stories. Through a blend of theory and hands-on application, you'll embark on a journey to uncover the stories hidden within your data sets. You'll master the art of design principles to craft aesthetically pleasing and informative visualizations that resonate with your audience. Finally, you'll learn the science of clear communication, ensuring your data visualizations are understood and actionable by diverse audiences.

By the end of this course, you'll be equipped to transform raw data into clear, concise, and impactful stories that drive informed decision-making.

Course Description

Data Visualization is the graphical representation of information. Data Visualization and related technologies create value within organizations by providing insight from complex sets of data by communicating key aspects therefrom. This course focuses on how to increase the likelihood of action based on insights from data by telling stories with data that leverage effective Data Visualizations. The course includes a mix of theory and hands-on application using the latest data visualization technologies and techniques.

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, students should be able to:

  • Articulate the interplay between art and science in creating effective data visualizations. Understand how visual design principles enhance data accuracy and clarity, fostering compelling storytelling.
  • Utilize contemporary data visualization tools to design and develop impactful narratives with data. This includes applying design principles to create aesthetically pleasing and informative visualizations.
  • Craft data visualizations tailored for different audiences. Consider the scientific principles of clear communication to ensure visualizations are readily understood and actionable.
  • Effectively present "stories with data" to a variety of audiences. This includes leveraging both the visual and informational aspects of data visualizations to engage and inspire action.
